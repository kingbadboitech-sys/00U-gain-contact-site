How to Use SCARLET MD

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Enter your WhatsApp number in the correct format (2348140825958)
  2. Click on "Generate Pairing Code" button
  3. Wait for the 8-digit code to be generated
  4. Open WhatsApp on your phone
  5. Go to Linked Devices → Link a Device
  6. Enter the 8-digit code when prompted
  7. Your WhatsApp will now be connected to SCARLET MD

Tip: For best results, use a stable internet connection and ensure your phone has an active internet connection during the pairing process.
